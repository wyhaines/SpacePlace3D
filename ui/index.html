Ship Controls

Desktop Controls:

  • Mouse: Click and drag to steer the ship (direction)
  • W / Up Arrow: Increase thrust
  • S / Down Arrow: Decrease thrust
  • Space: Brakes
  • T: Toggle travel mode (subluminal/superluminal)
  • E: Scan nearby objects

Mobile Controls:

  • Touch screen: Touch and drag anywhere to steer
  • Up Arrow Button: Increase thrust
  • Down Arrow Button: Decrease thrust
  • BRAKE Button: Press and hold to slow down
  • SCAN Button: Scan nearby objects for information
  • WARP Button: Toggle between travel modes

Status Information:

  • The Thrust Bar shows your current thrust level
  • Objects and players are labeled when near your ship
  • Your current position coordinates are shown at the bottom
  • The travel mode indicator shows if you're in subluminal or superluminal travel

Travel Modes:

  • Subluminal: Normal speed for precise navigation within star systems
  • Superluminal: Faster travel between star systems with reduced turning control
